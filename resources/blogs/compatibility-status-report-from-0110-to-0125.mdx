Hello and welcome to a brand new compatibility report article!.

This time we compare how shadPS4 emulator has crown up from 0.11.0 to 0.12.5 release

There were several fixes and optimizations in the last version , here we will try to make a compare of how shadPS4 has been improved in the last version based on the reports on our compatibility list. Note , that many more games probably are playable or even close to playable status , i am trying to cover as much as i can here since the list is probably quite big.

Some comments on the results

* Linux playable games has increased a lot , that mostly on 2 things. First , our testers seems to like linux more (and report more) . Secondly , ps4 is based on FreeBSD OS , which is closer to linux OS so emulating is more accurate.
* MacOS compatibility is low . There are several reasons around this . First , we don't have a lot of mac testers (interested?) . Secondly , macOS builds are not running natively , they are running through Rosseta2 which is an emulated x86\_64 environment, in the future who knows? maybe we will get an arm64 recompiler ;)
